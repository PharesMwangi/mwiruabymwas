About Mwirua Farmers

Mwirua Farmers Society ltd is a well established coffee society located in Kirinyaga county.

MISSION

To improve production of high quality and quantity coffee and other farm products for competitive world market prices by intensifying training and adopting of new technology embracing good agricultural practices and offering efficient and effective serices to the satisfaction of members.

VISION

To be a leading vibrant society in Kenya that will promote members social economic excellence and productivity by 2024.

SLOGAN

An Excellent Service For Prosperity

We have nine factories namely:

  • 01 - Kariaini
  • 02 - Mitondo
  • 03 - Getuya
  • 04 - Gathambi
  • 05 - Kiaragana
  • 06 - Kiabwe
  • 07 - Rwamuthambi
  • 08 - Riakiania
  • 09 - Ihara

    • Our factories perform activities such as:

    1. Receiving red ripe coffee berries from farmers
    2. Recording the cherry weight from every farmer according to their membership number
    3. Pulping the received cherry
    4. Washing and drying the pulped cherry
    5. Packaging and delivering the dry parchments to dry millers for processing and marketing.

    6. we also offer farming advisory services through our agronomist through:
      1. Announcements in churches and public barazas.
      2. Very active whatsapp group communications.
    7. Farm inputs are readily available in our chemical stores at affordable prices including:-
      1. Insecticides.
      2. Fungicides.
      3. Fertilizers.
      4. Lime and others.

    We are commited to process our members coffee using our high standard processing machineries observing high quality hygiene according to NEMA standards

    All our branches are functional from Monday to Friday 8:00 am - 5:00 pm and on Saturdays 8:00 am to 12:00 noon
